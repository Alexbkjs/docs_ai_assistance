# Resources

## Resource types

Resources are items from your Shopify markets that can be switched using the app. You can change their order or set custom items and names.

Note: This option is available on the Premium plan only.

Available resources in Shopify stores:

*   **Countries** - a list of countries added to your Shopify markets.
*   **Currencies** - a list of currencies corresponding to the countries added to your markets. By default, your store has only one currency in the primary market. To use the local currencies of the countries in your additional markets, the Shopify Payments option must be available in your store. You can find the list of supported countries on the [Supported countries for Shopify payments](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/payments/shopify-payments/supported-countries) page.
*   **Languages** - a list of languages added to your Shopify markets. The required languages must first be added under **Shopify settings** → **Languages** and assigned to the relevant markets.
*   **Market Domains** - a list of active markets on your store. Each market must point to a separate domain or subdomain to appear in the market domain list. For more details, see **Shopify settings** → **Domains**.

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/0b42aa3e-a347-41c6-b354-ba522dae7fa4/available-resources_szudlu.png)

Note: Market Domains work correctly only when each market points to a separate domain or subdomain.

The app also provides the option to add custom resources — for example, to point a language, currency, or country to a specific URL.

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/d5a28b0f-34a6-4646-9945-0392f0b47e6a/point-language-or-country_j1lqhg.png)

You can also set custom URLs for existing resources.

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/d9ad84cd-d7f9-4a8e-bbe4-b19b925cd885/custom-url-for-existing-resour_1hsy4l3.png)

To create your own custom resources, follow these steps:

1.  Click on the Resource list → **Add custom resource**.
2.  Enter the name of your resource and click **Apply**.
3.  You will be taken to the created resource page. To add items, click **Add resource** in the top-right corner.
4.  Enter the title and link of the resource, then click **Apply** and **Save**.

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/875817f0-3311-4d18-a231-b16f2febbd2f/add-custom-resource-test_e78b6w.png)

Once created, apply the custom resource to the required selector. Open the selector or banner → click on the Resource list and select your custom resource. **Save** the changes.

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/fdf0d25a-afb6-40dc-9179-b9bd9051a30c/add-resource-to-selector_jbbt2z.png)

## Additional resources

**Add third-party resources**

This option allows you to create a custom resource item with a specific URL.

*   Go to Resources → click **Add resource** → Add third-party resource.
*   Fill in the fields in the window that appears → click **Apply**.

**Add market resource**

Shopify market resources can only be added from within your Shopify store. When you click **Add market resource** in the app, you are redirected to the Markets section of your store.

*   Go to Resources → click **Add resource** → Add market resource.
*   You will be taken to the Markets page. Click **Add market** → enter the market name, select the markets you need, and click **Add market**.
