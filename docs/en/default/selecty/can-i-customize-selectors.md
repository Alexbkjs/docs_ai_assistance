# Customization

Yes. The app provides multiple customization options.

When you create a new selector, you are taken to the customization page where you can select the **theme, type, and color scheme**. We are continuously adding new themes and color schemes to give you the best options.

## Available themes

*   **Basic** - a standard button layout with a simple, intuitive interface.
*   **Material** - a fully responsive theme with a stricter design and colors.
*   **Cute** - a responsive design with smooth lines and appealing color schemes.
*   **Minimal** - a clean, simple design that keeps the focus on your products.

## Selector Types

The selector type determines how the list of languages unfolds on your customer's screen. The following types are available:

*   **Free plan:** Dropdown, Wheel, Inline, Modal.
*   **Pro and Premium plans:** Layered, Sidebar, Cascade, Scale, Tape-line.

## Color Schemes

The app offers a wide range of color schemes. You can choose a color scheme for each theme individually, and instantly preview how it looks across different themes.

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/23bdd81c-55a2-43ed-a36a-bc7e6a6a64c9/selector-color-schemes_xu6dyb.png)

You can also manually customize colors and fonts for each selector, or apply custom CSS.

The result is an attractive selector that matches your store design and allows visitors to choose the correct language or currency.
