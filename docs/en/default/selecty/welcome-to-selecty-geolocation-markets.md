# Welcome to Geolocation & Markets

Install [Geolocation & Markets](https://apps.shopify.com/selectors) to boost your store!

If your store already uses Shopify markets, the application helps you design switchers and a banner so that customers can easily switch to their native language and the currency of their country.

## Main benefits

✅ Unlimited language, currency, country, and market switchers — and combinations of all of them

✅ Templates, themes, and color schemes so selectors match your store design

✅ Recommendation banner with geolocation detection and personalized language suggestions

✅ Markets redirect

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/201b87ee-89a6-49fa-b610-1298685cc67e/selecty-dashboard_lq8jkg.png)

## How the app can help you

The application significantly simplifies the shopping experience for your international customers. It helps your store become easy to use for visitors from different parts of the world by providing convenient switchers.

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/19823fa6-1573-48b6-bc6a-4da276c56447/sidebar-selector_cys4z2.png)

Let your customers use a language selector to view your store in the most readable language, choose their native currency for international orders with a currency switcher, or navigate to another market with a market selector or country switcher. Enable the recommendation banner to use geolocation detection and offer personalized language and currency suggestions. Make your online store easy to understand for customers from all over the world.
