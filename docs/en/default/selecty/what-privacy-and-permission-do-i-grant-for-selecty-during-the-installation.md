# Scopes overview

## What are scopes?

By confirming the [app](https://apps.shopify.com/selectors) installation, you grant our application access to certain functionality. This guide explains why we request each scope.

## What this app can do in your store

**Store owner information**

The app requests access to your contact information (name, email address, phone number) and location (physical address). We use this information to communicate with store owners about important updates and announcements.

your market's current settings

**View Shopify admin**

The app requests this scope to access media files you upload to the store (for example, images of flags and currencies). This allows you to install custom resource icons and customize the [Market Recommendation banner](/selecty/can-i-offer-visitors-to-select-the-required-language-and-currency-based-on-their-location).

**View your Online Store**

The app requests this scope to access your store themes. We use this to view your current theme and respond to its settings.

**View other data**

Requests access to locales and Shopify Markets settings. We use this data to synchronize the app with your market's current settings.

A full list of active privacy and permission details is available in the app by clicking the three dots in the top right corner → **Manage app**. You will also see all privacy and permission details during the installation process when granting the required scopes.
