# Can I offer visitors to select the required language and currency based on their location?

  

# Add Recommendation Banner

  

Yes, of course. You can add a ****recommendation banner**** to allow customers to choose their preferred language and currency.

  

To add a banner to your store, open the app and scroll down until you find the section for the recommendation banner.

  

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/40ef06a4-540d-4125-abf0-e700b1aacd5d/markets-recommendations-select_1ox2ily.png)

  

Click on ****Customize**** and you will be moved to the banner page. Here you can customize banner and enable it.

  

For example, you can select [resource](/available-resources) - elements that your customers can switch with the help of Selecty. You can also set up type, color scheme, animation style, and customize text.

  

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/e5f5f4e5-1b30-45cd-9934-967ec2ec1461/markets-recommendations-settin_t7t5tw.png)

  

In our article about the [recommendation banner](https://help.devit.software/en/article/how-does-the-recommendation-banner-work-16cw9r6/) we explain how the Selecty app detects customer's language, currency, and country. Check it out.
