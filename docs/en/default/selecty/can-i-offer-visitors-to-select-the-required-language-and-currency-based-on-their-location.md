# Add Recommendation Banner

Yes. You can add a **recommendation banner** to allow customers to choose their preferred language and currency.

To add a banner to your store, open the app and scroll down to the recommendation banner section.

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/40ef06a4-540d-4125-abf0-e700b1aacd5d/markets-recommendations-select_1ox2ily.png)

Click **Customize** to go to the banner page, where you can configure and enable it.

For example, you can select a [resource](/selecty/available-resources) — the elements your customers can switch — and configure the type, color scheme, animation style, and text.

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/e5f5f4e5-1b30-45cd-9934-967ec2ec1461/markets-recommendations-settin_t7t5tw.png)

Our article about the [recommendation banner](https://help.devit.software/en/article/how-does-the-recommendation-banner-work-16cw9r6/) explains how the app detects a customer's language, currency, and country.
