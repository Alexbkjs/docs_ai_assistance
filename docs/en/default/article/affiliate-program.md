# ✔️ About the affiliate program

Our affiliate program allows you to earn up to **40%** in recurring commissions by referring Shopify merchants to our apps. We offer different plans that provide different conditions and commissions. Anyway, you will receive favorable terms of cooperation and will be able to earn by attracting new customers.

# ✔️ Benefits of an affiliate partnership

*   **High subscription fees**. All our plans provide favorable terms and high commissions.
    
*   **Flexible partnership.** Select the plan that is most comfortable for you and earn easily.
    
*   **Dedicated support.** We are always in touch and happy to help you with any questions.
    
*   **No commission limits.** The more clients you refer, the more you earn.
    
*   **Security.** We use a certified system, Mantle, and all operations are completely secure.

# ✔️ Commission structure

*   **Fast Track Affiliates:** 40% commission on total sales in the first 3 months.
    
*   **Growth Partner Affiliates:** 30% commission on total sales in the first 6 months.
    
*   **Sustainable Affiliates:** 20% commission on total sales in the first 12 months.
    
*   **Lifetime Revenue Partners:** 10% commission on total sales, forever!
    

Please note, that Mantle takes a 10% fee on the total payout to cover processing costs. For example, if you have $100 in your Mantle account, you'll receive $90 after deducting the Mantle fee. Refer to [this article](https://heymantle.com/docs/affiliates/payouts) for more details.

# ✔️ FAQ

**Is there a fee to join?**

No, there is no fees for joining the affiliate program. There are no upfront costs and no hidden fees. Just real earning potential.

**How much can I earn with your affiliate program?**

We provide different plans and you can earn up to 40% in commissions. Your earnings grow with the number and type of your referrals. The more customers you attract, the more your earnings will be.

**When is a referral considered successful?**

Referrals count as successful when the customer installs the app, finishes the trial without canceling, and we approve this installation. Important: Payments will be accrued only from the moment we confirm the referral claim.

**How can I track my referrals?**

You will get access to a dedicated affiliate dashboard. There you can track your clicks, conversions, and commissions in real time.

**When will I receive the money?**

Commissions are payable once your total affiliate earnings exceed $100. Important: Payments will be accrued only from the moment we confirm the referral claim.

Please note, that Mantle takes a 10% fee on the total payout to cover processing costs.

**How will I get my money?**

We process payments securely and send them straight to your account. Choose from multiple payment options for your convenience.

**Do you have any limitations for rewards?**

No, there are no limits on the number of referrals or the commissions you can earn. Refer as many customers as you wish. you'll earn commissions for each successful referral, as long as the customer stays subscribed and continues paying.

**Do you have custom terms for users with more than 10 active affiliates?**

Yes, of course! They can contact support ( [support@devit.software](http://email@email.email)) to improve the terms of their plan and receive bonuses.

**Where can I read Terms and Conditions?**

You can find our Affiliate Program Terms [here](https://devit.software/affiliate-terms). This document covers everything from eligibility and payment structure to commission rates and policies, ensuring complete transparency and clarity for our affiliates.

**What if I still have questions?**

If you still have questions, you can write us: [support@devit.software](http://email@email.email). We will be happy to tell you more about our program and answer all your questions.
