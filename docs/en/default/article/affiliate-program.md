# Affiliate Program

Our affiliate program allows you to earn up to **40%** in recurring commissions by referring Shopify merchants to our apps. We offer different plans with different conditions and commission rates. You'll receive favorable partnership terms and earn by bringing in new customers.

## Benefits of an affiliate partnership

*   **High commissions.** All our plans offer favorable terms and competitive commission rates.

*   **Flexible partnership.** Choose the plan that works best for you and start earning.

*   **Dedicated support.** We're always available to help you with any questions.

*   **No commission limits.** The more clients you refer, the more you earn.

*   **Security.** We use a certified system, Mantle, and all transactions are completely secure.

## What support we provide

You can always reach us at [support@devit.software](http://email@email.email). Our affiliate support team is here to answer your questions and provide guidance every step of the way.

## Commission structure

*   **Fast Track Affiliates:** 40% commission on total sales in the first 3 months.

*   **Growth Partner Affiliates:** 30% commission on total sales in the first 6 months.

*   **Sustainable Affiliates:** 20% commission on total sales in the first 12 months.

*   **Lifetime Revenue Partners:** 10% commission on total sales, forever.

Please note that Mantle takes a 10% fee on the total payout to cover processing costs. For example, if you have $100 in your Mantle account, you'll receive $90 after the Mantle fee is deducted. Refer to [this article](https://heymantle.com/docs/affiliates/payouts) for more details.

## FAQ

**Is there a fee to join?**

No, there are no fees for joining the affiliate program. There are no upfront costs and no hidden fees — just real earning potential.

**How much can I earn with your affiliate program?**

We offer different plans, and you can earn up to 40% in commissions. Your earnings grow with the number and type of your referrals. The more customers you attract, the more you earn.

**When is a referral considered successful?**

A referral is successful when the customer installs the app, completes the trial without canceling, and we approve the installation. Payments are accrued only from the moment we confirm the referral claim.

**How can I track my referrals?**

You will have access to a dedicated affiliate dashboard where you can track your clicks, conversions, and commissions in real time.

**When will I receive the money?**

Commissions are payable once your total affiliate earnings exceed $100. Payments are accrued only from the moment we confirm the referral claim.

Please note that Mantle takes a 10% fee on the total payout to cover processing costs.

**How will I get my money?**

We process payments securely and send them directly to your account. Choose from multiple payment options for your convenience.

**Do you have any limitations for rewards?**

No, there are no limits on the number of referrals or commissions you can earn. Refer as many customers as you wish — you'll earn commissions for each successful referral, as long as the customer stays subscribed and continues paying.

**Do you have custom terms for users with more than 10 active affiliates?**

Yes! Contact support at [support@devit.software](http://email@email.email) to discuss improved plan terms and bonuses.

**Where can I read the Terms and Conditions?**

You can find our Affiliate Program Terms [here](https://devit.software/affiliate-terms). This document covers everything from eligibility and payment structure to commission rates and policies, ensuring complete transparency for our affiliates.

**What if I still have questions?**

If you have additional questions, contact us at [support@devit.software](http://email@email.email). We'd be happy to tell you more about our program and answer any questions you have.
