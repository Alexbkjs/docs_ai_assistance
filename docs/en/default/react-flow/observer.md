# Observer

## What is the Observer feature?

Observer is used to **track updates** within customers, orders, products, and more. This option helps you monitor changes such as updates to product titles, tags, customer names, and other important fields.

Observer helps you **stay informed about important changes** without manual checks — ensuring you never miss a critical update in your store.

## How to track updated items?

**Example:** Track the addition of new tags with a specific value on products.

1.  Select the **Order updated** trigger.
2.  Click **Select** in the right-side menu → **Order** → **Tags**.

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/ccadb0cf-c25e-4524-a50a-ac5d0fcf0788/select-condition-order-updated_2fkw4e.png)

3.  Click the + icon in the trigger block and select **Condition**.
4.  In the right-side menu, click on the condition field.
5.  Scroll until you find the **Observe** option.
6.  Click on it and select **Added items**.

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/df2c5015-81e6-4619-ac83-e9b6431499a5/observe-added-items_1io78ew.png)

7.  Click **Select matcher** → is equal → set the required value.

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/5438843b-3091-49d7-a2ee-4c91908e242d/set-value_2yuuk4.png)

You can add more conditions or actions to the flow as needed. When you've finished configuring, click **Save** in the upper-right corner.
