# FAQ

## Can I share my screen and use a camera while using Huddles?

Yes, both options are available to you.

## Does the bot record the call?

No, Huddles does not record calls.

## How does a Huddle call work?

The bot creates a temporary voice channel that all channel members can enter.

## Do I need to remove the voice channel manually?

No, the voice channel is deleted automatically when everyone leaves the channel, or after 5 minutes if no one has entered.

## Can I use Huddles in any channel of my server?

Yes, you can use Huddles in any channel. Just invite the bot and follow the instructions in this article.

## If I need help with Huddles, how can I get it?

If you have any questions, please contact our support team: [support@devit.software](mailto:support@devit.software).

## Where can I leave feedback about the bot?

You can leave your feedback on the [Huddles](https://top.gg/bot/1123558266555871252) page.
