# How to use Huddles

<iframe type="text/html" width="560" height="349" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/iWuy8xn3I3Y?rel=0" loading="lazy" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture; web-share" style="position: absolute; top: 0px; left: 0px; width: 100%; height: 100%; border: 0px;"></iframe>

With Huddles, you can start a call in any Discord channel. [Invite the bot](https://top.gg/bot/1123558266555871252) to your server and follow the instructions below.

There are two ways to start a call in Discord through Huddles:

1.  Open the channel where you want to make a call, click **+** on the left side of the text field → **Use Apps** → select **Huddles** → `/start`, and press Enter.

2.  Open the channel where you want to make a call, type `/start` in the text field, and press Enter.

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/57462321-28ef-41ad-850c-dbda02c0b9b0/huddles-bot_a2mvza.png)

The bot generates a call start notification and sends it to all channel members, inviting them to join. Members can follow the call link or join via the Huddles category in the left-side menu.

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/b1134269-c697-4b2e-9d91-7fc5ed57a4d4/meeting_j69mbf.png)

After the call ends, the bot automatically deletes the voice channel, and the Huddles category becomes empty.

If the bot sends a link to join the call but no one follows it, all channel members will receive a reminder after 30 seconds.

If no one joins the call within 5 minutes, the voice channel is automatically deleted.
