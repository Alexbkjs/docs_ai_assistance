## What is Email bot?

[Email Bot](https://top.gg/bot/1106165894859735070) is a tool that generates a unique email address for you, which you can use to subscribe to important notifications from any service. All messages sent to this address are forwarded and displayed in the Discord channel you have connected.

## Main benefits

*   **Subscribe to important notifications** – integrate the bot and receive notifications directly in your Discord channel.
*   **Easy setup** – set up the bot in a few simple steps.
*   **Unlimited integrations** – the bot provides a unique email address for every integration, so you can create as many integrations as you need.

## Examples of integrations

*   Send various alerts to the #random channel — for example, server downtime, weather events, traffic closures, domain renewal reminders, and more.
*   Send contact form submissions from your website to the #support or #hr channels.
*   Send greetings to the #random channel — for example, holiday messages for your colleagues.
