# Free Shipping Progress Bar

The **Free Shipping Progress Bar** widget motivates customers to add more items to their order by visually showing how close they are to unlocking free shipping. This simple yet effective tool encourages higher cart values and boosts overall sales.

## Add widget

1.  Open the funnel and move to the Checkout page step.
2.  Click **Add widget** -> Free shipping bar.
3.  In the right menu, configure the appropriate settings: set the content type, text, minimum amount, and colors.
4.  Click **Save** in the upper right corner.
