# Quick start in ReSell

Install [Post Purchase Upsell—ReSell](https://apps.shopify.com/resell) to boost your store!

## Get started with ReSell App

To start funnels running and increasing profits in your Shopify store, you need to select ReSell in your checkout settings.

1.  From your Shopify admin, click **Settings**.
2.  Click **Checkout**.
3.  In the **Post-purchase page** section, select the ReSell app.

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/3484bdaa-cdb8-466c-a07d-924113f92aba/settings-checkout_1fpi4kz.png)

You can find more detailed information at [Shopify](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/checkout-settings/checkout-style#set-post-purchase-app).

## Create your first funnel

Here is a closer look at how this feature works.

When a customer completes a purchase in your store, you can use the **Thank You page** to display a **post-purchase upsell** — offering related products or a special deal to encourage an additional purchase.

### Step 1. Create a funnel

1.  On the **Dashboard** or **Funnels** tab, click the **Create funnel** button.
2.  Select how you want to create the funnel: AI template or Manually.

*   **AI template** — an offer created automatically. You can edit this funnel and publish it immediately.
*   **Manually** — a manually created offer. (Instructions below.)

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/ab1fe83a-cb65-471a-b5a8-5184827123f2/create-the-first-funnel_1gxg5r6.png)

### Step 2. Set the display conditions

1.  Select the trigger that will launch the funnel:

*   **Certain product** — triggers the funnel when a customer selects a specific product from your store.
*   **Product from collection** — triggers the funnel when a customer buys any product from the chosen collection.
*   **Any product** — applies the funnel to all products in your store.

2.  Click **Choose product** and select the product that will trigger this funnel.
3.  Select an appropriate condition to make the funnel more personalized (optional).
4.  Click **Next step**.

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/b2d46bc4-9131-47b4-9f72-2daa081d713a/display-when_1u9ileo.png)

### Step 3. Checkout (Shopify Plus only)

This option is **available for Shopify Plus** plans only. If you use a Basic or another Shopify plan, this step will be hidden. Here you can select products to offer customers during the checkout process **before** payment.

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/6de866a9-21dc-4d04-bafb-2632f5431001/checkout-shopify-plus-plan_zxsf0g.png)

### Step 4. Post-purchase offer

Click **Add offer** and select the product (you can also select a collection or the Any option).

You will see three available offers. Use all of them or just one. If you use all three, the display sequence is as follows:

First, **Upsell Offer #1** is shown.

*   If the customer accepts it, **Upsell Offer #2** appears.
*   If the customer declines, a **Downsell Offer** is shown instead.

This way, you can present up to **two additional products** to each customer.

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/521e51e1-5a98-47a1-a318-66e0b4441074/post-purchase-offer_5624kj.png)

Note: you can click **Edit offer** to add different widgets, such as Add-on Services and Free Shipping Progress Bar.

### Step 5. Thank you page upsell offer

ReSell also lets you configure a Thank you page upsell offer — display sales offers, provide coupons and special promotions, and encourage customers to make another purchase.

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/12ec85a7-4c58-4c9c-afe5-beeab811bf57/thank-you-page-offer_x0aoct.png)

### Step 6. POS

If you have the POS app, this step will be available. It lets you add additional products during the checkout process at a POS terminal.

### Step 7. Order Status Page

On this step, you can offer customers additional products, collections, or automatic offers on the Order Status page after checkout.

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/b2276b4f-2ff4-4803-900c-5cd50707a943/order-status-page_me7t52.png)

### Step 8. Publish the funnel

Click the **Publish** button to launch the funnel. If you'd like our support team to review your funnel setup, select the appropriate option.
