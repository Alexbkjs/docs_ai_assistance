# Re-enter Payment Information?

No, customers do not need to re-enter their payment information.

One-click upsell is available for **Post-purchase** offers. When a customer accepts an offer, the product is added and charged automatically.

On the **Thank you page**, customers may need to enter payment details if the offer is processed as a separate order. In that case, the purchase is completed and charged independently.
