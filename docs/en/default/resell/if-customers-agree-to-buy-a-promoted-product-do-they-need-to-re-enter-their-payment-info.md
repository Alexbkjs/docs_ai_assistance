# If customers agree to buy a promoted product, do they need to re-enter their payment info?

  

# Re-enter Payment Information?

  

No, they don’t need to re-enter their payment information.

  

One-click upsell is available for ****Post-purchase****. When a customer accepts an offer, the product is added and charged automatically.

  

On the ****Thank you page****, customers may need to enter payment details if the offer is processed as a separate order. In this case, the purchase is completed and charged independently.
