The ****Add-on Services**** option allows you to offer extra services or complementary products directly on the Checkout and Post-purchase pages. It helps ****increase the average order value**** by suggesting relevant add-ons that enhance the main purchase and improve the customer’s experience.

# ✔️ Types of Add-on services

*   ****Shipping protection**** - covers lost, damaged, or stolen packages, helping shoppers feel more confident about their purchase and improving trust in your store.
*   ****Gift wrapping**** is perfect for holidays and special occasions making the shopping experience more personal.
*   ****Personal offer**** — a product you select from your store’s existing catalog and promote as a special bonus.

# ✔️ Ways to add Add-on services to your offer

### Add an Add-on offer

1.  On the Checkout or Post-purchase step, click ****Add offer**** and click ****Add-on****.

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/a6a90188-6fb3-4761-972e-a42273997833/add-add-on_1ot1nq0.png)

2.  Choose the type of add-on service: ****Shipping protection**** or ****Gift wrapping****.
3.  In the ****Configuration**** section select the suitable option.

*   ****Automatic offer creation**** - the app will automatically choose suitable products.
*   ****Select existing product**** - manually choose a specific product to offer.

4.  In the ****Price**** section, set the amount for the selected service.
5.  Click ****Save****.

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/17a2695a-37a6-4845-850a-abdd59ec0b21/add-on-offer_1wczj5j.png)

Note: You can add the same type of add-on offers to both the Checkout and Post-purchase pages. If a customer accepts the offer during Checkout, it will automatically be hidden on the Post-purchase step.

### Add widget

On the Checkout and Post-purchase steps you can edit your offers and add add-on widgets:

1.  Open the funnel and move to the Checkout or Post-purchase step.
2.  Click ****Edit offer****.
3.  Click ****Add widget**** -> Add-on services.
4.  In the right menu make the appropriate settings: set the type of the service, set title and description, checkbox text and price.
5.  Click ****Save**** in the upper right corner.

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/e423237c-66c8-4572-9e17-83fcd23f5ba0/customize-add-on_1fgi8wp.png)
