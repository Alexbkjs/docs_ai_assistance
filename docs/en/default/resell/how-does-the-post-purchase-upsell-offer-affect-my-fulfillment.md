# How does an offer affect fulfillment?

The upsell offer does not create a new order — it edits the original order. The prices are combined, and customers do not need to pay twice.

According to Shopify's rules, the upsell offer creates a [fulfillment hold](https://shopify.dev/changelog/post-purchase-fulfillment-holds) for [one hour](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/orders/fulfillment/fulfilling-orders/fulfill-your-own?shpxid=7509cf43-B4CB-4673-D187-ED402046F424#fulfillment-holds). This means the order fulfillment status is set to **On hold** while the customer is presented with the upsell offer. The hold lasts for one hour, giving the customer time to return and complete or decline the offer.
