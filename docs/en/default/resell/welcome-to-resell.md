Want to increase your Shopify profits? [ReSell](https://apps.shopify.com/resell) helps you turn every purchase into a new sales opportunity. Explore the key features and benefits below.

# ✔️ Benefits of ReSell App:

*   Build automatic sales & marketing campaigns.
*   Offer additional products after checkout.
*   Increase revenue by raising the average order value.
*   Set flexible conditions to personalize each offer.
*   Increase purchases with additional discounts and coupons.
*   Track performance to optimize your funnels.
*   Automates your post-purchase offers.

# ✔️ How can ReSell App help you?

After a customer completes a purchase, a standard Shopify ****Thank you page**** appears - this is the moment when many entrepreneurs are likely missing out on additional revenue opportunities.

The Thank you page is a prime opportunity to offer your customer a related product, helping to ****increase your average order value****. And with ReSell, you can turn this page into a revenue driver by showing relevant post-purchase offers!

Create funnels for specific products or categories, and let ReSell automatically display personalized upsells that increase your average order value and overall profits.

# ✔️ How does ReSell App work?

*   The ****Dashboard**** gives you a quick overview of your app activity and performance.
*   The ****Funnels**** tab allows you to create ****automated flows**** and customized post-purchase upsell offers. Use the AI template or create them Manually.
*   In the ****Analytics**** tab you can ****track your results****, including offer views, declines, revenue, and conversions.
*   The ****Settings**** section allows you to configure the ****automatic removal of the unpaid upsell product**** from order and the option of processing of the Thank you page offer as a separate order or adding it to the existing one.
*   Resell also provides an opportunity to ****add translation**** to post-purchase offers and you can do this in the ****Localization**** tab.
