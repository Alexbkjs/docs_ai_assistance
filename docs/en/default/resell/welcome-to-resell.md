# Welcome to ReSell

Want to increase your Shopify profits? [ReSell](https://apps.shopify.com/resell) helps you turn every purchase into a new sales opportunity. Explore the key features and benefits below.

## Benefits of ReSell App

*   Build automatic sales and marketing campaigns.
*   Offer additional products after checkout.
*   Increase revenue by raising the average order value.
*   Set flexible conditions to personalize each offer.
*   Drive more purchases with additional discounts and coupons.
*   Track performance to optimize your funnels.
*   Automate your post-purchase offers.

## How can ReSell help you?

After a customer completes a purchase, a standard Shopify **Thank you page** appears — this is the moment when many store owners miss out on additional revenue.

The Thank you page is a prime opportunity to offer a related product and **increase your average order value**. With ReSell, you can turn this page into a revenue driver by showing relevant post-purchase offers.

Create funnels for specific products or categories, and let ReSell automatically display personalized upsells that increase your average order value and overall profits.

## How does ReSell work?

*   The **Dashboard** gives you a quick overview of your app activity and performance.
*   The **Funnels** tab lets you create **automated flows** and customized post-purchase upsell offers. Use an AI template or build them manually.
*   The **Analytics** tab lets you **track your results**, including offer views, declines, revenue, and conversions.
*   The **Settings** section lets you configure **automatic removal of unpaid upsell products** from orders and control whether Thank you page offers are processed as separate orders or added to the existing one.
*   ReSell also supports **adding translations** to post-purchase offers via the **Localization** tab.
