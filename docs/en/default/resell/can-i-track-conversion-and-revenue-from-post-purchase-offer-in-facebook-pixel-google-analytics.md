# Add Facebook Pixel & Google Analytics script

Yes. Shopify lets you integrate additional services to improve your online store.

To track customer data and behavior, you can add Facebook Pixel and Google Analytics to your store.

Make sure you have a [Facebook Pixel](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/952192354843755) and a [Google Analytics account](https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/9304153#account) set up before proceeding.

1.  Open your Shopify store and click **Settings**.

2.  Click **Checkout**, then scroll to the **Post-purchase page** section. In the **Additional scripts** field, add the necessary scripts.

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/2c914411-2cbf-4eb3-a674-20d8ed720052/additional-scripts_1udv3g7.png)

For more detailed information, refer to the [Shopify Help Center](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/promoting-marketing/analyze-marketing/pixel-tracking-post-purchase-page), which includes a step-by-step guide on adding pixel tracking to the post-purchase page.

3.  Click **Save**.
