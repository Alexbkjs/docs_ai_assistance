# Can I track conversion and revenue from post purchase offer in Facebook Pixel & Google Analytics?

  

# Add Facebook Pixel & Google Analytics script

  

Yes, sure! Shopify allows its clients to use additional services that can improve their online stores.

  

To track customer data and behaviour on your online store you can add Facebook Pixel and Google Analytics.

  

Make sure that you have [Facebook Pixel](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/952192354843755) and [Google Analytics account](https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/9304153#account).

  

1.  Open your Shopify store and click on Shopify ****Settings****.

  

2.  Click on ****Checkout**** > scroll to the ****Post-purchase page**** section. In the ****Additional script**** section add necessary scripts.

  

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/2c914411-2cbf-4eb3-a674-20d8ed720052/additional-scripts_1udv3g7.png)

  

For more detailed information, you can refer to the [Shopify Help Center](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/promoting-marketing/analyze-marketing/pixel-tracking-post-purchase-page). There you will find a step-by-step guide on how to add pixel tracking on the post-purchase page.

  

3.  ****Save**** changes.
