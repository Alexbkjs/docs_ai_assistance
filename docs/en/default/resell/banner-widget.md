# Banner widget

  

In this article:

  

*   [Add widget](#1-add-widget)
*   [Advanced setup](#1-advanced-setup)

  

****Banner widget**** allows you to highlight special offers, sales, or announcements with an eye-catching banner. With its help, you can grab your customers’ attention and guide them toward key products or promotions.

  

Available on Checkout, Post-purchase, Thank you page, and Order status pages.

  

# ✔️Add widget

  

1.  Open the funnel and move to the step where you want to add the widget.
2.  Click ****Edit offer****.
3.  In the left side menu, click ****Add widget**** -> Banner widget.
4.  In the right menu, make the appropriate settings: set the type of content, set text, a minimal amount, and colors.

  

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/979f65fd-314a-4a41-a732-cf8a6c6d854c/banner-widget_erp0f2.png)

  

5.  Click ****Save**** in the upper right corner.
