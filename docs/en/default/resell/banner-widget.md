# Banner widget

The **Banner widget** lets you highlight special offers, sales, or announcements with an eye-catching banner. Use it to grab your customers' attention and guide them toward key products or promotions.

Available on Checkout, Post-purchase, Thank you page, and Order status pages.

## Add widget

1.  Open the funnel and move to the step where you want to add the widget.
2.  Click **Edit offer**.
3.  In the left side menu, click **Add widget** -> Banner widget.
4.  In the right menu, configure the appropriate settings: set the content type, text, minimum amount, and colors.

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/979f65fd-314a-4a41-a732-cf8a6c6d854c/banner-widget_erp0f2.png)

5.  Click **Save** in the upper right corner.
