# Vaulted payment processors

## What are vaulted payment processors?

Vaulted payment processors are payment gateways that securely store (or "vault") customer payment details for future transactions. This enables merchants to process payments without requiring customers to re-enter their payment information each time they make a purchase.

This functionality is essential for enabling features such as one-click upsells, recurring subscriptions, and a faster, frictionless checkout. By securely retaining payment credentials, vaulted payment processors help businesses improve conversion rates and customer retention while maintaining compliance with industry security standards.

## Approved vaulted payment processors

You have the flexibility to use different vaulted payment processors, but you must ensure they are supported by Shopify. We recommend the following approved options:

*   [Shopify Payments](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/payments/shopify-payments)
*   [Bankful Native](https://bankful.com/shopify-native/)
*   [Adyen Payments](https://docs.adyen.com/plugins/shopify/)
*   [Authorize.net](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/payments/third-party-providers/set-up-authorize-net)
*   [Cybersource ICL App](https://www.cybersource.com/en-us/why-cybersource/partners/partner-marketplace/ecommerce/shopify.html)

## How to enable vaulted payment processors

1.  Open your store and go to **Settings**.
2.  Click **Payments** and select the vaulted payment processor.
3.  Follow the setup instructions for your selected payment processor.
