# Can I show additional offers based on the customers' location?

# Show Offer By Country

  

Yes, ReSell allows you to display offers based on the customer’s location. You can show specific products to customers from selected countries and restrict offers by shipping regions if needed.

  

1.  Click on ****Add condition**** on the first step when you create a funnel
2.  Shipping -> ****Restrict by country****.
3.  Select the country that you need.
