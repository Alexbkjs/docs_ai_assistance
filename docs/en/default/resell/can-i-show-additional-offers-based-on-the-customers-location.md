# Show Offers by Country

Yes, ReSell lets you display offers based on the customer's location. You can show specific products to customers from selected countries and restrict offers by shipping region if needed.

1.  Click **Add condition** on the first step when creating a funnel.
2.  Go to Shipping -> **Restrict by country**.
3.  Select the country you want to target.
