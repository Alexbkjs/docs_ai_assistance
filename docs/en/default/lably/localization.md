# Localization

Lably makes it easy to localize your labels and badges for different languages and markets.

When your badges are in the customer's native language, it's easier for them to navigate your site, understand your offers, and make confident buying decisions.

To add a translation, follow these steps:

1.  Open the label and click **Label** in the left-side menu.
2.  Click **Locales** next to the text field.
3.  Select a language.
4.  Manually enter the translation.
5.  Click **Apply**.

To learn more about the app's features, see the [Conditions](/lably/conditions) article.
