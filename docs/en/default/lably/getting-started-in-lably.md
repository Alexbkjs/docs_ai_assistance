# Quick Start in Lably

## About the Lably app

The [Lably - Product Labels & Badges](https://apps.shopify.com/lably) app lets you add eye-catching, customizable badges and labels to your products, highlight specific items, and motivate customers to make more purchases in your store.

**Labels** are displayed on the product image; **badges** are displayed in the product content.

## Steps to add your first label or badge

1.  Open the app and click **Add new**.
2.  Select an option: label or badge. Choose **Add new label** to get started.

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/2d6ee77c-bed5-4147-95f2-7b5af78dcdd9/select-label_tdoijb.png)

## Customization features

The app provides multiple customization options to help you tailor and display badges exactly as needed.

The left-side menu contains all available options:

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/1f4ea351-fff2-463e-b980-43b2232619c4/label-customization-options_juukw9.png)

*   **Label / Badge** — choose the type of your label or badge:

    *   Select **Image** to upload your own image or use pre-designed thematic product labels.

    *   Select **Text** to set custom text in the text field.

Note: You can [add translation](/lably/localization) to your badges and labels.

![](https://cdn.heymantle.com/docs/screenshots/721d3914-5657-4261-9c3c-4721ee6bd933/label-type-text_1b4i3v7.png)

*   **Styles** — customize your label: set its shape, font, size, style, and more.
*   **Position** — set the label position and margin.
*   **Link** — enter a URL to redirect customers to a specific product or page when they click the label.
*   **Animations** — select the animation type for your label.
*   **Week schedule** — choose the day and time when your label appears in your store. By default, all options are enabled, meaning the label displays continuously until you delete it. You can set custom times for each day separately.
*   **Visibility on pages** — select the pages where you want labels to appear. For example, selecting Home page means the label appears only on the Home page. When a customer navigates to the product page, the label will not be shown there.
*   **Display period** — plan the display window for your label. Disable "All time" and select the period when you want it to appear.
*   **Display conditions** — apply labels to specific products based on rules.

## Advanced Setup

In addition to the basic settings, the app offers advanced options. You can configure [display conditions](/lably/conditions), [add translations](/lably/localization) to labels and badges, and use [custom fonts](/lably/how-to-use-a-custom-font-in-a-label) and [custom selectors](/lably/can-i-apply-custom-selectors).
