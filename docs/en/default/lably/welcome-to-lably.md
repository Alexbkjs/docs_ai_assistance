# Welcome to Lably - Product Labels & Badges!

Make your products stand out and boost conversions with eye-catching labels and badges — fully customizable and easy to set up with [Lably](https://apps.shopify.com/lably).

## Key features

*   **Template library**

Choose from a library of stylish, pre-designed labels and badges. Use them as-is or customize them to fit your brand perfectly.

*   **Conditions**

Set conditions for when a label or badge is displayed — such as for new arrivals, sale items, or specific customer locations.

*   **Scheduling**

Control when your labels appear. Schedule them by day and time to align with promotions or campaigns.

## Why choose Lably?

Lably helps you create and manage custom product labels and badges with powerful options and full design flexibility.

*   **Flexible customization:** Adjust fonts, colors, size, shape, position, text, animations, and more. Make each label uniquely yours.
*   **Display period:** Set start and end dates to automatically control how long labels stay visible.
*   **Page visibility control:** Choose where labels appear — on product pages, collections, or across your entire store.
*   **Conditions:** Set up rules to display labels based on product type, inventory levels, location, tags, and more.

## How Lably helps you sell more

**Grab attention** by highlighting key products with attractive labels — like "New" or "Bestseller".

Use promotional badges like "50% Off" to **boost conversions**.

**Build trust** with badges such as "Organic" or "Eco-Friendly" to reinforce product value and credibility.

Labels like "Only 2 Left" or "Ends Today" encourage **quicker purchases** and increase average order value.

Running **seasonal sales** or limited-time promotions? Lably helps align your store visuals with your marketing efforts.
