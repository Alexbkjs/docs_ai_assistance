# Welcome to Lably - Product Labels & Badges!

Make your products stand out and boost conversions with eye-catching labels and badges - fully customizable and easy to set up with [Lably](https://apps.shopify.com/lably).

# ✔️ Key features

*   ****Template library****

Choose from a library of stylish, pre-designed labels and badges. Use them as-is or customize to fit your brand perfectly.

*   ****Conditions****

Set conditions when the label or badge will be displayed - like for new arrivals, sale items, or specific customer locations.

*   ****Scheduling****

Control when your labels appear. Schedule them by day and time to match promotions or campaigns effortlessly.

# ✔️ Why choose Lably?

Lably helps you create and manage custom product labels and badges with powerful options and full design flexibility.

*   ****Flexible customization:**** Adjust fonts, colors, size, shape, position, text, animations, and more. Make each label uniquely yours.
*   ****Display period:**** Set start and end dates to automatically control how long labels stay visible.
*   ****Page visibility control:**** Choose where labels appear - on product pages, collections, or across your entire store.
*   ****Conditions option:**** Set up rules to display labels based on product type, inventory levels, location, tags, and more.

# ✔️ How Lably helps you sell more

You can ****grab attention**** by highlighting key products with attractive labels - like “New”, or “Bestseller”.

Use promotional badges like “50% Off” to ****boost conversions****.

****Build trust**** with badges such as “Organic”, “Eco-Friendly” to reinforce product value and credibility.

Labels like “Only 2 Left” or “Ends Today” encourage ****quicker purchases**** and increase average order value.

Run ****seasonal sales**** or limited-time promotions? Lably helps align your store visuals with your marketing efforts.
